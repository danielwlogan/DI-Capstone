About Yelp Star Adjust

The Data

Yelp Star Adjust is a result of the 6th Yelp Dataset Challenge. The data consists of 1.6 million reviews of 61 thousand businesses from thousands reviewers. These businesses are located around the world.

  • Canada: Montreal and Waterloo
  • Germany: Karlsrhue
  • Scotland: Edinburgh
  • United States: Champaign-Urbana, Charlotte, Las Vegas, Madison, Phoenix and Pittsburgh
