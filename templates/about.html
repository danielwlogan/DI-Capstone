About Me

Hi, I am Daniel Logan. I received my PhD in astronomy from the University of Massachusetts. I have always been interested in how things are measured and if the measures we have are telling us what we want to know. Star Adjust is my capstone project when I was a fellow at the Data Incubator and grew out of this interest.

I am entering the field data science because I see the tools it brings as a new way to address some of these measurement challenges. I am currently exploring opportunities to develop my Data Science skills. If you have questions or comments, please email me at the link below or feel free to check me out on LinkedIn.
